Tattoo Aftercare Best Practices

Proper aftercare is crucial for healing and maintaining your new tattoo. Here's a quick guide: Keep it wrapped for 2-4 hours, then wash gently with unscented soap. Apply a thin layer of fragrance-free moisturizer 2-3 times a day. Avoid sun exposure, swimming, and picking at scabs. If issues arise, consult a doctor.
